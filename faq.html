Improbidade administrativa

Q. Is improbidade civil or criminal? → pitfalls.md "Civil vs criminal"; topics/improbidade.md §1.

Q. What changed with Lei 14.230/2021? → topics/improbidade.md §2; jurisprudencia-stf.md §1 (Tema 1199 retroactivity).

Q. What are the three categories of improbidade? → topics/improbidade.md §1: enriquecimento ilícito (LIA.9), prejuízo ao erário (LIA.10), violação de princípios (LIA.11).

Q. Who has standing to bring improbidade actions after 2021? → LIA.17: only the Ministério Público (post-Lei 14.230/2021). Public entities lost initiating standing.

Q. Is improbidade ressarcimento subject to prescription? → topics/improbidade.md §1: STF Tema 897 holds that ressarcimento for dolosa improbidade is imprescritível; declaratory and sanction components remain subject to prescription.

Mayoral accounts and Ficha Limpa

Q. How does Ficha Limpa work? → topics/contas-municipais.md §3; topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md §7. Statute: LI.1.I.g amended by LFL.

Q. Who decides if a mayor's accounts are rejected for Ficha Limpa purposes? → The câmara municipal, not the TCE. See topics/contas-municipais.md §§2–3 and pitfalls.md "Mayoral accounts". STF Tema 157.

Q. What's the 2/3 rule on contas? → CF Art. 31 §2: the câmara needs a 2/3 supermajority to override the TCE parecer prévio. Following the parecer requires only simple majority. See topics/contas-municipais.md §§1, 6.

Q. What's the difference between contas de governo and contas de gestão? → topics/contas-municipais.md §2; pitfalls.md "Mayoral accounts". STF ADPF 982.

Electoral system and procedure

Q. How does the open-list PR system work? → topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md §2.

Q. What are coligações and federações? → topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md §3. Coligações for proportional elections were abolished by EC 97/2017 (effective 2020); federações replaced them with multi-year commitments.

Q. What is the cláusula de desempenho? → topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md §4. EC 97/2017 introduced it; thresholds tighten 2018 → 2022 → 2026 → 2030.

Q. How is campaign finance regulated? → topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md §6. Corporate donations were banned by STF ADI 4650 (2015); FEFC public fund created by Lei 13.487/2017.

Q. What's the difference between AIME, AIJE, AIPRC, and RCED? → topics/processo-eleitoral.md §§4–7; pitfalls.md "Electoral procedures".

Q. How does Brazil count campaign finance? → topics/prestacao-contas-eleitorais.md, topics/processo-eleitoral.md §2.

Q. When are mayoral elections held? What's the second-round threshold? → topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md §1. Two rounds apply only in municipalities with more than 200,000 registered voters.

Q. What is the Ministério Público Eleitoral? → Not a separate branch — federal MP members (Procuradores da República, etc.) act as MP eleitoral when assigned to electoral functions.

Procurement

Q. What's the current procurement law in Brazil? → topics/licitacoes.md. L14133 is current; transition from L8666 ended 30 December 2023.

Q. What are the procurement modalities? → topics/licitacoes.md §"Modalities". Concorrência, tomada de preços, convite, concurso, leilão (Lei 8.666); pregão (Lei 10.520); diálogo competitivo (Lei 14.133).

Q. What is dispensa de licitação? → topics/licitacoes.md §"Dispensa de licitação". L8666.24, L14133.75.

Q. How does CADE handle bid-rigging cartels? → topics/licitacoes.md §"CADE and cartel enforcement"; CADE Guia de Combate a Cartéis em Licitação (2019) for detailed typology.

Q. What are common bid-rigging patterns? → topics/licitacoes.md §"CADE typology": cover bidding, bid suppression, blocking, rotation, market division.

Q. What are the criminal penalties for procurement fraud? → topics/anticorrupcao-penal.md §1, topics/licitacoes.md. Historically Lei 8.666/93 Arts. 89–98; now CP Arts. 337-E to 337-P (post-Lei 14.133/2021).

Anti-corruption

Q. What are the parallel regimes for prosecuting corruption in Brazil? → topics/anticorrupcao-penal.md §7 (matrix), topics/improbidade.md §4. Criminal (CP, Lei 8.137, lavagem, orcrim), civil (improbidade), administrative (CADE, Lei Anticorrupção), fiscal (TCU/TCE), electoral (Ficha Limpa).

Q. What is the Lei Anticorrupção? → topics/anticorrupcao-penal.md §5. Lei 12.846/2013, corporate liability, objective standard, leniency agreements with CGU.

Q. How does an acordo de leniência work? → topics/anticorrupcao-penal.md §5; topics/cgu-controle-interno.md §4.

Q. What is COAF and what is a RIF? → topics/anticorrupcao-penal.md §2. COAF is the financial intelligence unit (now at BACEN); RIFs are financial intelligence reports.

Q. What are CEIS and CNEP? → topics/cgu-controle-interno.md §4. Public registries of firms barred from contracting (CEIS) and firms sanctioned under Lei Anticorrupção (CNEP).

Federalism and fiscal

Q. How is municipal revenue distributed in Brazil? → topics/federalismo-fiscal.md §2. FPM, FPE, ICMS quota-parte, FUNDEB, SUS transfers, royalties.

Q. What is FPM and how is it calculated? → topics/federalismo-fiscal.md §2 ("FPM"). Step function of population; 16 brackets for interior municipalities. Used as a canonical RD instrument.

Q. What does the LRF require of municipalities? → topics/federalismo-fiscal.md §4. Personnel spending limits (60% of RCL for municipalities), debt limits, last-year-of-mandate restrictions, fiscal reporting (RREO, RGF).

Q. What is the reforma tributária (EC 132/2023)? → topics/federalismo-fiscal.md §1 ("Reforma tributária"). Replaces ICMS, ISS, IPI, PIS, COFINS with CBS + IBS + IS. Phase-in 2026–2033.

Q. Where is municipal fiscal data published? → SIOPS (health), SIOPE (education), SICONFI (general), FINBRA (legacy). See topics/federalismo-fiscal.md §5 and topics/transparencia-dados.md.

Labor courts

Q. What changed with the 2017 reforma trabalhista? → topics/justica-trabalho.md §"Filing costs". Filing costs introduced, justiça gratuita tightened, no-show penalties, etc.

Q. Why did labor court filings drop after 2017? → topics/justica-trabalho.md §"Impact on filing volumes". The reform produced one of the sharpest filing-cost shocks in any major federal reform.

Q. What did STF ADI 5766 do? → topics/justica-trabalho.md §"STF ADI 5766"; jurisprudencia-stf.md §3. Partially struck down 2017 reform provisions for justiça gratuita beneficiaries.

Q. What is the homologação procedure for extrajudicial settlements in labor courts? → topics/justica-trabalho.md §"Settlement observability". CLT Arts. 855-B–E (added by Lei 13.467/17).

Q. Why are most labor settlements observable in Brazilian data? → topics/justica-trabalho.md §"Settlement observability — a data advantage". Indisposable rights doctrine + mandatory court mediation push settlements through the courts.

Audit and control

Q. What is the difference between CGU and TCU? → pitfalls.md "CGU"; topics/cgu-controle-interno.md, topics/tribunais-contas.md. CGU is internal control (Executivo); TCU is external control (reports to Congress).

Q. What was the CGU random municipal audit program? → topics/cgu-controle-interno.md §3; quasi-experimentos.md §1. Programa de Fiscalização por Sorteios, 2003–2015. Canonical RD.

Q. How does TCE-SP audit municipalities? → topics/tribunais-contas.md. Audesp data system, parecer prévio on contas de governo, direct judgment of contas de gestão.

Q. How is the relator assigned at TCE-SP? → topics/tribunais-contas.md §"Relator assignment". Random electronic draw with two-year exclusion rule for contas processos.

Transparency and data

Q. What is LAI? → topics/transparencia-dados.md §1. Lei de Acesso à Informação (Lei 12.527/2011).

Q. How does LGPD interact with court data? → topics/transparencia-dados.md §§2, 4. Judicial transparency has its own regime; LGPD applies but with public-interest legal basis.

Q. Can researchers process personal data without consent under LGPD? → topics/transparencia-dados.md §2 ("Research-specific exemption"). Yes, under LGPD.7 IV (research entities) and LGPD.11 II c (sensitive data for research).

Q. Where is federal procurement data published? → topics/transparencia-dados.md §3. Comprasnet (compras.gov.br), Painel de Compras.

Q. Where can I get federal expenditure data? → topics/transparencia-dados.md §3. Portal da Transparência (CGU). For municipal/state data, SICONFI (STN).

CNJ and judicial administration

Q. What does CNJ do? → topics/cnj-administracao-judicial.md §1. Administrative and disciplinary oversight of the judiciary; normative power via Resoluções; publishes Justiça em Números.

Q. What is Justiça em Números? → topics/cnj-administracao-judicial.md §2. Annual statistical report on the Brazilian judiciary.

Q. What are the Tabelas Processuais Unificadas? → topics/cnj-administracao-judicial.md §3. CNJ Resolução 46/2007. Mandatory unified case-classification schema.

Q. What is execução fiscal and why is it 35% of pending cases? → topics/cnj-administracao-judicial.md §7. Tax enforcement, the single largest source of judicial congestion.

Empirical research

Citations and the article DB

Q. How do I cite a specific article in the prose? → CLAUDE.md "Citing statutes". Use the compact backtick form apelido.artigo.path, e.g., LIA.10.§1, L8666.23.I.a, LE.36-A.

Q. How do I look up an exact statutory text? → tools/leis_artigos/README.md. Use cite.py (backtick form) or lookup.py (positional args).

Q. Why doesn't my citation resolve? → pitfalls.md "Citations and database lookups". Possible reasons: law not cataloged, path doesn't exist, article number wrong, alínea uses old concatenated form.

Q. What apelidos are available? → leis_index.yaml. Search for apelido: to find all cataloged laws.

Q. How do I find which file discusses a law? → leis_index.yaml discussed_in: field, or grep the directory for the law number.
