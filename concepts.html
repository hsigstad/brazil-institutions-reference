Brazilian Institutions Topics Laws Concepts

Institutional concepts

Named institutions, doctrines, and reform episodes. Project sites cite these as I:slug tokens, which render as the concept's title and link back here.

audiência unaI:audiencia-una
The labor courts' single unified hearing — conciliation, defense, evidence, and judgment in one session; fractionated into 2-3 sessions in practice.
Conselho Nacional de JustiçaI:cnj
The national judicial-oversight council created by EC 45/2004 — administrative supervision, disciplinary power, and binding normative resolutions.
impedimentoI:impedimento
Objective grounds requiring a judge's recusal (kinship, prior involvement); recognised ex officio, nullity if violated (CPC.144).
Justiça do TrabalhoI:justica-do-trabalho
Brazil's specialised federal labor judiciary — Varas do Trabalho, 24 regional TRTs, and the national TST.
2017 labor reformI:labor-reform-2017
Lei 13.467/2017 — introduced filing costs and honorários de sucumbência and tightened justiça gratuita, reshaping labor-litigation incentives.
2021 improbidade reformI:lia-reform-2021
Lei 14.230/2021 — rewrote the Lei de Improbidade Administrativa, eliminating culpable improbidade and requiring proof of dolo.
2015 civil procedure reformI:ncpc-reform
Lei 13.105/2015, in force March 2016 — the current Code of Civil Procedure (NCPC), replacing the 1973 code.
2019 Pacote AnticrimeI:pacote-anticrime
Lei 13.964/2019 — criminal-justice reform package; introduced the juiz das garantias and tightened plea-bargain and sentencing rules.
labor-court recusal rulesI:recusal-impedimento
How impedimento operates in the labor courts — who must recuse, how the conflict is raised, and where a recused case goes.
2004 Reforma do JudiciárioI:reforma-do-judiciario
EC 45/2004 — the largest structural reform to the judiciary since 1988; created the CNJ, repercussão geral, and súmula vinculante.
CNJ resolutionsI:resolucoes-cnj
Normative acts of the CNJ, binding across the judiciary — the instrument behind e.g. Resolução 200/2015 on impedimento.
settlement observability in labor courtsI:settlement-observability
Nearly all labor-court settlements are judicially mediated and observable in the case data — a measurement advantage unusual internationally.
suspeiçãoI:suspeicao
Subjective grounds for recusal (friendship, enmity, financial interest); must be raised by a party, unlike impedimento (CPC.145).
