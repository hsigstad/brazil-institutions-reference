# FAQ — common research questions, mapped to topical files

A navigation aid: common questions a researcher (or Claude) might ask
about Brazilian institutions, with pointers to where the answer lives.
This file does **not** answer the questions in detail — for that, follow
the link.

**Topics / keywords**: faq, questions, navigation, index, where to look,
how to find.

**How to use**: grep this file for keywords matching your question. The
matched entries point to the topical file (and section) where the
answer lives.

## Improbidade administrativa

**Q. Is improbidade civil or criminal?**
→ [`pitfalls.md`](pitfalls.md) "Civil vs criminal"; [`topics/improbidade.md`](topics/improbidade.md) §1.

**Q. What changed with Lei 14.230/2021?**
→ [`topics/improbidade.md`](topics/improbidade.md) §2; [`jurisprudencia-stf.md`](jurisprudencia-stf.md) §1 (Tema 1199 retroactivity).

**Q. What are the three categories of improbidade?**
→ [`topics/improbidade.md`](topics/improbidade.md) §1: enriquecimento ilícito (`LIA.9`), prejuízo ao erário (`LIA.10`), violação de princípios (`LIA.11`).

**Q. Who has standing to bring improbidade actions after 2021?**
→ `LIA.17`: only the **Ministério Público** (post-Lei 14.230/2021). Public entities lost initiating standing.

**Q. Is improbidade ressarcimento subject to prescription?**
→ [`topics/improbidade.md`](topics/improbidade.md) §1: STF Tema 897 holds that ressarcimento for **dolosa** improbidade is **imprescritível**; declaratory and sanction components remain subject to prescription.

## Mayoral accounts and Ficha Limpa

**Q. How does Ficha Limpa work?**
→ [`topics/contas-municipais.md`](topics/contas-municipais.md) §3; [`topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md`](topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md) §7. Statute: `LI.1.I.g` amended by `LFL`.

**Q. Who decides if a mayor's accounts are rejected for Ficha Limpa purposes?**
→ The câmara municipal, not the TCE. See [`topics/contas-municipais.md`](topics/contas-municipais.md) §§2–3 and [`pitfalls.md`](pitfalls.md) "Mayoral accounts". STF Tema 157.

**Q. What's the 2/3 rule on contas?**
→ CF Art. 31 §2: the câmara needs a 2/3 supermajority to **override** the TCE parecer prévio. Following the parecer requires only simple majority. See [`topics/contas-municipais.md`](topics/contas-municipais.md) §§1, 6.

**Q. What's the difference between contas de governo and contas de gestão?**
→ [`topics/contas-municipais.md`](topics/contas-municipais.md) §2; [`pitfalls.md`](pitfalls.md) "Mayoral accounts". STF ADPF 982.

## Electoral system and procedure

**Q. How does the open-list PR system work?**
→ [`topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md`](topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md) §2.

**Q. What are coligações and federações?**
→ [`topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md`](topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md) §3. Coligações for proportional elections were abolished by EC 97/2017 (effective 2020); federações replaced them with multi-year commitments.

**Q. What is the cláusula de desempenho?**
→ [`topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md`](topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md) §4. EC 97/2017 introduced it; thresholds tighten 2018 → 2022 → 2026 → 2030.

**Q. How is campaign finance regulated?**
→ [`topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md`](topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md) §6. Corporate donations were banned by STF ADI 4650 (2015); FEFC public fund created by Lei 13.487/2017.

**Q. What's the difference between AIME, AIJE, AIPRC, and RCED?**
→ [`topics/processo-eleitoral.md`](topics/processo-eleitoral.md) §§4–7; [`pitfalls.md`](pitfalls.md) "Electoral procedures".

**Q. How does Brazil count campaign finance?**
→ [`topics/prestacao-contas-eleitorais.md`](topics/prestacao-contas-eleitorais.md), [`topics/processo-eleitoral.md`](topics/processo-eleitoral.md) §2.

**Q. When are mayoral elections held? What's the second-round threshold?**
→ [`topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md`](topics/partidos-e-sistema-eleitoral.md) §1. Two rounds apply only in municipalities with more than 200,000 registered voters.

**Q. What is the Ministério Público Eleitoral?**
→ Not a separate branch — federal MP members (Procuradores da República, etc.) act as MP eleitoral when assigned to electoral functions.

## Federalism and fiscal

**Q. How is municipal revenue distributed in Brazil?**
→ [`topics/federalismo-fiscal.md`](topics/federalismo-fiscal.md) §2. FPM, FPE, ICMS quota-parte, FUNDEB, SUS transfers, royalties.

**Q. What is FPM and how is it calculated?**
→ [`topics/federalismo-fiscal.md`](topics/federalismo-fiscal.md) §2 ("FPM"). Step function of population; 16 brackets for interior municipalities. Used as a canonical RD instrument.

**Q. What does the LRF require of municipalities?**
→ [`topics/federalismo-fiscal.md`](topics/federalismo-fiscal.md) §4. Personnel spending limits (60% of RCL for municipalities), debt limits, last-year-of-mandate restrictions, fiscal reporting (RREO, RGF).

**Q. What is the reforma tributária (EC 132/2023)?**
→ [`topics/federalismo-fiscal.md`](topics/federalismo-fiscal.md) §1 ("Reforma tributária"). Replaces ICMS, ISS, IPI, PIS, COFINS with CBS + IBS + IS. Phase-in 2026–2033.

**Q. Where is municipal fiscal data published?**
→ SIOPS (health), SIOPE (education), SICONFI (general), FINBRA (legacy). See [`topics/federalismo-fiscal.md`](topics/federalismo-fiscal.md) §5 and [`topics/transparencia-dados.md`](topics/transparencia-dados.md).

## Labor courts

**Q. What changed with the 2017 reforma trabalhista?**
→ [`topics/justica-trabalho.md`](topics/justica-trabalho.md) §"Filing costs". Filing costs introduced, *justiça gratuita* tightened, no-show penalties, etc.

**Q. Why did labor court filings drop after 2017?**
→ [`topics/justica-trabalho.md`](topics/justica-trabalho.md) §"Impact on filing volumes". The reform produced one of the sharpest filing-cost shocks in any major federal reform.

**Q. What did STF ADI 5766 do?**
→ [`topics/justica-trabalho.md`](topics/justica-trabalho.md) §"STF ADI 5766"; [`jurisprudencia-stf.md`](jurisprudencia-stf.md) §3. Partially struck down 2017 reform provisions for *justiça gratuita* beneficiaries.

**Q. What is the homologação procedure for extrajudicial settlements in labor courts?**
→ [`topics/justica-trabalho.md`](topics/justica-trabalho.md) §"Settlement observability". CLT Arts. 855-B–E (added by Lei 13.467/17).

**Q. Why are most labor settlements observable in Brazilian data?**
→ [`topics/justica-trabalho.md`](topics/justica-trabalho.md) §"Settlement observability — a data advantage". Indisposable rights doctrine + mandatory court mediation push settlements through the courts.

## Citations and the article DB

**Q. How do I cite a specific article in the prose?**
→ [`CLAUDE.md`](CLAUDE.md) "Citing statutes". Use the compact backtick form ``apelido.artigo.path``, e.g., ``LIA.10.§1``, ``L8666.23.I.a``, ``LE.36-A``.

**Q. How do I look up an exact statutory text?**
→ [`tools/leis_artigos/README.md`](tools/leis_artigos/README.md). Use `cite.py` (backtick form) or `lookup.py` (positional args).

**Q. Why doesn't my citation resolve?**
→ [`pitfalls.md`](pitfalls.md) "Citations and database lookups". Possible reasons: law not cataloged, path doesn't exist, article number wrong, alínea uses old concatenated form.

**Q. What apelidos are available?**
→ [`leis_index.yaml`](leis_index.yaml). Search for `apelido:` to find all cataloged laws.

**Q. How do I find which file discusses a law?**
→ [`leis_index.yaml`](leis_index.yaml) `discussed_in:` field, or grep the directory for the law number.

## Adding to this FAQ

When you find a question that should be here but isn't:

1. Phrase the question naturally (as a researcher would ask it).
2. Identify the file(s) that answer it.
3. Add an entry under the appropriate section (or create a new section).
4. Keep the answer to one line — link to the file for the substance.

This is a navigation aid, not a textbook. The goal is "fastest path
from question to answer", not "answer in place".
